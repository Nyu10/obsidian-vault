### INvesting


if i was serious about it

- **Deep Research:** Pick one sector (like data centers, lithium mining, or specialized infrastructure) and write a short "white paper" for yourself on why it’s undervalued or why it fits your long-term view.
    
- **Track Your "System":** Use a simple spreadsheet to track why you bought something, your "thesis" (the reason you bought it), and your exit criteria. This turns investing into a data-driven process rather than just guessing.
    
- **Backtesting/Modeling:** Use your skills to look at historical data. How did those commodities perform during the 2008 crash? How did REITs hold up when interest rates were at 5%? Finding the answers to these questions is far more engaging than just watching a ticker move.
