WHAT IS A PROVERB? A proverb (Hebrew masal) is a poetic, terse, vivid,
thought-provoking saying that conveys a world of truth in a few words.

Modern people do not have a category for proverbs. They are neither
absolute commands nor promises, and often they are partial. That is, they
need to be put beside other proverbs on the same subject to get the full
picture. They are observations about how life works. The point of a
proverb, then, is to get rightly related to reality through hard thinking and
sustained reflection. A proverb is like hard candy: If you just bite down on
it, you get little out of it and may even get a broken tooth. Instead you must
meditate on it until the sweetness of insight comes.
